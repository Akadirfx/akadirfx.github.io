How It Works

Our streamlined process bridges the gap between AI concepts and real-world products.

  1. 1. Develop Your Concept with AI: Use ChatGPT, DeepSeek, or other AI tools to flesh out your initial product idea.
  2. 2. Submit Your Concept: Upload your AI-generated concept, sketches, or descriptions through our form.
  3. 3. Engineering Review: We analyze your concept for feasibility and suggest improvements.
  4. 4. Refinement & Development: We refine the design, create technical drawings, or develop prototypes.
  5. 5. Finalize & Protect: We help prepare your product for manufacturing and assist with patents.
